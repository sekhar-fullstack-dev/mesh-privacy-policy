Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Mesh ("we", "our", or "us") is a proximity-based social app that uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to help you discover content from people physically nearby. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. You may also set a display name and bio within the app.

Content you create: Posts, stories, and messages you create are stored on our servers so they can be fetched by nearby devices.

Bluetooth data: The app broadcasts and scans lightweight BLE signals containing only content identifiers (post IDs, story IDs) and a time-to-live counter. No personal information is transmitted over BLE. BLE signals are used solely for local proximity discovery and are never sent to our servers.

Camera: The camera is used only when you choose to create a story. Photos and videos are processed on-device and uploaded only if you explicitly post them.

Device information: We collect basic device identifiers for push notification delivery (via Firebase Cloud Messaging) and crash diagnostics.

Usage analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used in aggregate (screen views, feature usage). This data is anonymised and not linked to your identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Mesh service
  • To deliver push notifications (new messages, interactions)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To enforce our Terms of Service

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your content and account data are stored on secure cloud servers. Data in transit is encrypted using TLS. Direct messages between users are encrypted end-to-end using the Noise XX protocol before leaving your device.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the Settings screen, which permanently deletes your profile, posts, and messages from our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Mesh uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Firebase (Analytics, Cloud Messaging)

6. Children's Privacy

Mesh is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
